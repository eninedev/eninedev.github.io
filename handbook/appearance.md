# [Employee Handbook](index.md) • Professional Appearance

## Purpose

This policy informs employees about the need for projecting a clean and professional appearance and guidelines to enable every employee to meet the requirements.

## Dress Code

2.1. The Company maintains a business casual working environment. 

2.2. Employees, regardless of gender, must ensure that they are dressed in a professional manner. 

2.3. "Smart casual" is the minimum acceptable dress code at 1E9 Advisors. 

2.4. Footwear: Men must wear closed-toed shoes to the office. All other shoes including sandals, chappals, crocs, etc. are prohibited.

_1E9 Advisors reserves the right to continue, extend, revise or revoke this policy at its discretion._
