# [Employee Handbook](index.md) • Leave Policy

## Summary

1.1. The Company provides the following leave for all regular employees:
- Official Holidays (9)
- Earned Leave (15)
- Sick Leave (10)

## Official Holidays

2.1. The company provides 9 official holidays every year

- Holidays that fall on Saturday will be taken on the last business day before Saturday, usually Friday.
- Holidays that fall on Sunday will be taken on the first business day after Sunday, usually Monday.

2.2. Fixed day holidays are those that occur on a fixed day and are:

- New Years Day (Jan 01)
- Republic Day (Jan 26)
- Independence Day (Aug 15)
- Gandhi Jayanti (Oct 02)

2.3. Variable day holidays that change year to year and will be defined at the end of the prior year

- Holi
- Ganesh Chaturti
- Dussehra
- Diwali - Day 1
- Diwali - Day 2

2.4. Holiday Schedule

- [2021 - 2025](holidays/2021-2025.md)
- [2026 - 2030](holidays/2026-2030.md)
 
## Earned Leave

3.1. Employees will earn 1.25 days of earned leave every month, for a total of 15 days of annual leave per calendar year.

3.2. Probation Period

- Employees accrue earned leave during their Probation Period.
- Employees cannot avail earned leave during their Probation Period.

3.3. Notice Period

- Employees may not avail of earned leave while serving out their Notice Period.

3.4. Any leave taken without applying and receiving approval will be treated as Loss of Pay (LOP). LOP will have an impact on the employees' performance ratings and approvals.

3.5. Employees can carry over up to 5 days of earned leave into the following calendar year.

3.6. Upon termination of employment, any remaining balance of vacation shall be encashed to the exiting employee at the employee's Basic Pay rate at the time of exit.

3.7. Planned Leave: 

- Applications for planned leave must be made in advance
- Any vacation of up to 3 days must be made at least 8 days in advance.
- Any vacation of 4 days or more must be made at least one calendar month in advance.
- Applications for leave made with less than described notice may be treated as unplanned.
- Planned leave without approval of your manager and HR will be treated as unplanned.

3.8. Unplanned Leave: 

- Leave that is due to circumstances that are unplanned, including sickness and urgent personal work.
- Any unplanned leave will be treated as paid leave the first time every month and will be deducted from EARNED LEAVE.
- Any unplanned leave after the first day of unplanned leave in a given month will be treated as unpaid and be treated as LOP.
- Sick leave taken after the allowed number of Sick Days will be treated like all other unplanned leave in (b) and (c).

3.9. Leave cannot be taken just before and/or immediately after a long weekend. The company reserves the right to allow for exceptions at its discretion and only if an application is made more than 30 days in advance.

## Sick Leave

4.1. All regular employees will receive ten (10) sick days ("Sick Days")

4.2. An employee availing a Sick Day must inform their manager no later than 8a of the day they are scheduled to start work that day.

4.3. Sick Days may not be carried over into the following year.
